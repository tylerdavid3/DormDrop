NEU Housing & Dining Rates Research
====================================
Source: housing.northeastern.edu, dining.northeastern.edu, research via third-party aggregators
Academic Year: 2025–26 (2026–27 rates not yet published as of April 2026)

ROOM RATES (per year = 2x semester)
-------------------------------------
Economy Double/Triple (Stetson, Willis, Speare):        $10,270/yr  ($5,135/semester)
Standard Double (International Village / East Village): $11,360/yr  (~$5,680/semester)
Standard Triple (International Village):                $10,900/yr  (~$5,450/semester)
Suite room — multi-occupancy (West Village):            $12,580/yr  ($6,290/semester)
Standard Single:                                        $14,200/yr  (~$7,100/semester)
Suite room — single occupancy (West Village):           $15,000/yr  (~$7,500/semester)
Apartment shared room (no dining req.):                 ~$12,000/yr
Apartment single bedroom (no dining req.):              ~$15,000/yr

Average annual room charge (reported):                  ~$11,360/yr

MEAL PLANS (per year = 2x semester)
--------------------------------------
NU-Unlimited (most common freshman plan):   $8,900/yr  ($4,450/semester)  — includes $400 dining dollars + 10 guest passes
NU-225:                                     $8,900/yr  ($4,450/semester)  — includes $600 dining dollars + 10 guest passes
NU-180:                                     $7,870/yr  ($3,935/semester)  — includes $300 dining dollars + 10 guest passes
NU-150:                                     $6,930/yr  ($3,465/semester)  — includes $200 dining dollars + 10 guest passes
NU-100:                                     $5,600/yr  ($2,800/semester)  — includes $200 dining dollars + 10 guest passes
Profiler (non-traditional, $500/semester):  $1,000/yr  — does NOT satisfy freshman requirement

MEAL PLAN REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------
- All first-year students in university housing MUST enroll in a traditional meal plan
- First-years in meal-plan-required housing: auto-enrolled in NU-Unlimited (can switch to NU-225 or NU-180)
- First-years in non-meal-plan-required housing: auto-enrolled in NU-180 (can switch up)
- Apartment-style residences: no meal plan required

FEES & DEPOSITS
----------------
New student enrollment deposit:        $600 (nonrefundable)
Continuing student housing deposit:    $200/semester
Residence Student Association fee:     $37/semester ($19 half-semester)

Reported combined room+board average:  ~$20,260/yr
